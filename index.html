About

Welcome to the official homepage of the Dalian Analysis and Differential Equation Group at Dalian University of Technology. Our research focuses on analysis, differential equations, nonlinear partial differential equations, dynamical systems, and related mathematical theories and applications. This website provides information about our research, publications, members, and academic activities.
